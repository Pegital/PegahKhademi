This workshop aimed to interact with a multi-objective evolutionary algorithm to include designer taste more in the design process which I believe is a step closer for a user to be involved in these kinds of processes. The subject is designing a tower in Sydney.
▾

My subjective aim is to have more towers where the upper third box is dominant in its proportion compared to the rest and also emphasize rotation integrated into one side but exciting rotation overall. We were the first users of the Snowflake plugin which allows us to interact in the evolutionary process in 3 different ways:
1-Characteristic interaction
1) Box three-volume to the total volume
2) Rotation
