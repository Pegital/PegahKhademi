For this project, I am cooprating with a master student of cognitive \science who is working on ADHD specifically. We tried to provide children with assistive devices for their tasks.
Idea case study: Habitica star chart ,book reading reward
▾

The goal is to provide a system which allows users
to choose and construct their own character,
but for this level, I designed two characters,
and I codded them in Arduino to make them.
The prototype is for 18 tasks, and at each level,
the character achieves a prize, and it also has memory
which saves the level.
